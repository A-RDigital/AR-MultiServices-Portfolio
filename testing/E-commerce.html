My Product Selection Criteria

Data-driven approach to minimize risk and maximize profitability:

✅ Green Light Products
  • Price: $25–$120 (ideal impulse buy range)
  • Weight: Under 2 lbs (keeps FBA fees low)
  • Reviews: Less than 500 on page 1 (easier to rank)
  • Margins: 25%+ after all fees
  • Demand: 3,000+ monthly searches
  • Seasonality: Year-round appeal (not holiday-dependent)
❌ Hard Pass Products
  • Breakables: Glass, ceramics (high damage rate)
  • Restricted: Supplements, adult items (approval headaches)
  • Seasonal: Christmas decor (cash flow gaps)
  • High Returns: Clothing, jewelry (25%+ return rates)
  • Oversaturated: Phone cases, yoga mats (10K+ competitors)
  • Dangerous: Batteries, aerosols (shipping restrictions)
