WordPress Development FAQs

How long does it take to develop a custom WordPress website?

The timeline varies based on complexity. A basic WordPress site takes 2-4 weeks, while complex eCommerce solutions may require 8-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines after understanding your specific requirements.

What's the difference between a WordPress theme and template?

A theme controls the overall design of your WordPress site, while templates are page layouts within a theme. I create custom themes with multiple templates tailored to different content types.

Can you migrate my existing website to WordPress?

Yes! I specialize in seamless migrations from various platforms to WordPress, ensuring no data loss and maintaining SEO rankings. I handle everything from content transfer to URL redirects.

How do you ensure my WordPress site is secure?

I implement multiple security layers including SSL certificates, firewalls, regular updates, and security plugins. My development process follows WordPress security best practices to minimize vulnerabilities.

Do you provide WordPress training?

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive training sessions to help you manage your WordPress site. Training covers content updates, plugin management, basic troubleshooting, and best practices.

What's included in your WordPress maintenance plans?

My maintenance includes core/plugin updates, security monitoring, daily backups, performance optimization, uptime monitoring, and technical support. Plans are customizable based on your needs.

Can you integrate third-party services with WordPress?

Yes, i regularly integrate payment gateways, CRMs, marketing tools, and other services with WordPress. I use APIs and custom development to ensure seamless integration with your existing systems.

How do you optimize WordPress for search engines?

My SEO approach includes technical optimization (speed, mobile-friendliness), content strategy, schema markup, proper URL structures, and integration with tools like Yoast SEO. We follow Google's best practices.
