Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my digital marketing services.

How long does it take to see results from SEO?

SEO is a long-term strategy, and results typically start becoming noticeable within 3-6 months. However, this timeline can vary based on your industry competition, website age, and the current state of your SEO. I usually see initial improvements in rankings within the first 1-2 months, with more significant traffic growth occurring in months 3-6 and beyond.

What's the difference between SEO and PPC?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) focuses on improving your website's organic (unpaid) search rankings through content optimization, technical improvements, and link building. PPC (Pay-Per-Click) involves paying for ads that appear at the top of search results. SEO delivers long-term sustainable results, while PPC provides immediate traffic but stops when you stop paying. The most effective digital marketing strategies often combine both approaches.

How do you measure the success of a digital marketing campaign?

I measure success through key performance indicators (KPIs) that align with your business goals. These may include website traffic, conversion rates, lead generation numbers, sales figures, return on ad spend (ROAS), keyword rankings, engagement metrics, and more. Before starting any campaign, i establish clear benchmarks and goals to accurately measure progress and success.

Do you work with local businesses or only large corporations?

I work with businesses of all sizes, from local shops to national brands. My strategies are tailored to each client's specific needs, budget, and goals. For local businesses, I focus heavily on local SEO, Google My Business optimization, and geo-targeted advertising. For larger companies, i implement comprehensive digital marketing strategies across multiple channels.

What makes your digital marketing services different?

My approach combines technical expertise with creative strategy. Unlike many agencies that specialize in just one area, i offer integrated solutions that cover all aspects of digital marketing. i also prioritize transparency, providing detailed reporting and regular communication. Our team stays ahead of industry trends to ensure our clients benefit from the latest strategies and technologies.
